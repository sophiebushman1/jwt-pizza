## Root cause

Chaos was intentionally injected at the JWT Pizza Factory level as part of deliverable 11. The factory began rejecting orders and returning a report URL in its error response. The `orderRouter.js` in jwt-pizza-service correctly surfaces this URL in both success and failure responses via the `followLinkToEndChaos` field.

## Resolution

Navigated to the `followLinkToEndChaos` URL provided in the failed order response. This notified the factory to end the chaos. Subsequent orders were fulfilled successfully, confirming the incident was resolved.

No changes to jwt-pizza-service code were required.
